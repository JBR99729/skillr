Useful Resources

Explore our collection of free educational resources designed to support learning at home and in the classroom. These printable worksheets and learning tools cover a range of essential literacy and numeracy skills, including phonics, handwriting, counting, multiplication, number sense, skip counting, and problem-solving. Suitable for students, parents, teachers, tutors, and homeschool educators, these resources provide extra practice and reinforcement across key learning areas. Browse the resources below to find helpful printable activities that support confidence, skill development, and academic success.

Phonics Mapping: Write sounds in individual squares.

Practise phonics sound mapping with this free printable worksheet. Students listen to words and write individual sounds (phonemes) into separate boxes, helping to develop phonemic awareness, spelling skills, sound-letter relationships, and early reading confidence. Ideal for classroom, homeschool, and literacy intervention activities.

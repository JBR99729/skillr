Preschool & Foundation Worksheets

Parent & Teacher Tips

These printable preschool and Foundation worksheets are designed for early learners aged 3–5 years. They support simple, calm and confidence-building practice across maths, science and early learning skills.

For best results, use one worksheet at a time and keep practice sessions short. Around 15–20 minutes is usually enough for this age group.

To reduce paper waste, print only when needed or allow children to write answers on plain paper. If you cannot find a worksheet for a topic, please contact us at skillrhublearning@gmail.com .

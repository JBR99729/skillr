Parent & Teacher Tips

These printable preschool and Foundation worksheets are designed for early learners aged approximately 3–5 years. They support calm, simple and confidence-building practice.

Use one worksheet at a time and keep practice sessions short. Around 15–20 minutes is usually enough for this age group.

To reduce paper waste, print only the pages you need or allow children to record answers on plain paper.

Suggested Mathematics Year Plan

One possible sequence for covering the Foundation Mathematics curriculum.

Numbers to 20, Subitising, Counting and Comparing

Term 2

Part-Part-Whole, Addition and Subtraction, Equal Sharing and Grouping

Term 3

Repeating Patterns, Measurement Attributes, Time and Sequencing

Term 4

Shapes, Position and Location, Data and Statistics

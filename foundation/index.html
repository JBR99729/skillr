Pre-School(3-5yrs) /Foundation level Worksheets

Parent & Teacher Tips 1. Minimise screen distractions** For the best learning experience, encourage children to avoid using electronic devices for at least **20 minutes before starting** the worksheet. A calm, distraction-free environment can help them focus on the activity.

2. Carefully designed learning activities** Each Skillr worksheet is carefully selected and designed to encourage thinking, problem-solving, creativity and confidence while supporting key learning outcomes.

3. Print only when needed** To help reduce paper waste, consider having children write their answers on plain paper with a pencil instead of printing every worksheet. Handwriting practice also helps develop fine motor skills and supports the learning process.

4. One worksheet a day is plenty** For children aged **3–5 years**, attention spans are often around **15–20 minutes**. We recommend completing **one worksheet per day** and taking regular breaks to keep learning enjoyable. Consistent practice over time is more effective than rushing through many worksheets.

5. Need help?** If you have any questions or cannot find a worksheet for a particular topic, we'd love to help. Please contact us at **[skillrhublearning@gmail.com](mailto:skillrhublearning@gmail.com)** and we'll do our best to assist or prioritise your request.Happy learning!

Counting Numbers

Explore our collection of free printable counting worksheets and number activities for Pre-School and Foundation students. These resources help children develop early numeracy skills through counting practice, number recognition, number sequencing, ten frames, skip counting, and number sense activities. Ideal for parents, teachers, childcare centres, and early learning classrooms, these printable worksheets support confidence with numbers and build strong foundations for future mathematical learning.

Skillr Education

Skillr Education provides free printable Maths, Science and educational worksheets for students from Pre-School to Year 12. Our curriculum-aligned resources are designed to support teachers, parents and students with engaging, structured learning activities for use at home and in the classroom.

Our worksheets are organised by year level to make it easy to find suitable resources. Please note that year names vary between countries. For example, our Pre-School resources may also be suitable for children in Pre-K, Kindergarten, Foundation, Reception or Prep, depending on your local education system.

We are continually expanding our collection of worksheets. If you need resources for a particular topic that are not yet available, please contact us at skillrhublearning@gmail.com with the year level, subject, topic and curriculum information. We will do our best to prioritise your request.

A small note: Skillr Education is run by a small team on a part-time basis. While we take great care to respond to requests and continually add new resources, we appreciate your patience as we balance this project with our other commitments. We are committed to growing the library over time and are grateful for your support.

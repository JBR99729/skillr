Year 3 Worksheets

Explore our collection of free printable Year 3 worksheets designed to support learning at home and in the classroom. Our curriculum-aligned resources help teachers, parents and students build confidence through engaging activities and structured practice.

If you need worksheets for a particular topic, please contact us at skillrhublearning@gmail.com. We are a small team working part-time, but we will do our best to prioritise useful worksheet requests.

Parent & Teacher Instructions

For the best learning experience, encourage children to avoid electronic devices for at least 20 minutes before starting a worksheet. A calm, distraction-free environment can help children focus better.

Skillr worksheets are carefully selected to encourage thinking, problem-solving and confidence. These activities are designed to support classroom learning, home practice and early skill development.

Please print only when necessary. Children can also write answers on plain paper with a pencil, which supports handwriting practice and helps them engage more actively with the task.

We recommend completing no more than one worksheet per subject each day. Short, consistent practice is often more effective than rushing through many worksheets at once.

If you need help or cannot find a worksheet for a specific topic, please contact us at skillrhublearning@gmail.com. We are happy to help where possible.

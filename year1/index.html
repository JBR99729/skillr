Year 1 Worksheets

Explore free printable Year 1 worksheets for Maths and Science. These resources are designed for teachers, parents and students to support classroom learning, home practice and skill development.

If you need worksheets for a particular topic, please contact us at skillrhublearning@gmail.com. We are a small team working part-time, but we will do our best to prioritise useful worksheet requests.

Maths

Browse Year 1 Maths worksheets by topic.

S.No Topic Description Download
1Counting & Place ValueBuild number confidence with counting, reading, writing and ordering numbers. Students practise place value using tens and ones.
2Addition & SubtractionPractise simple addition and subtraction using pictures, number lines and number sentences. These worksheets support early arithmetic fluency.
3Number BondsDevelop understanding of how numbers can be split and combined. Activities include making ten, part-part-whole models and related facts.
4Skip CountingPractise counting by 2s, 5s and 10s using number patterns and visual activities. Skip counting supports early multiplication readiness.
5PatternsIdentify, continue and create repeating patterns. Pattern worksheets help develop sequencing, logic and early algebra thinking.
6MeasurementCompare length, mass and capacity using informal units and everyday objects. Students use words such as longer, shorter, heavier and lighter.
7TimeIntroduce days, months, calendars and simple clock reading. Students practise sequencing events and recognising o’clock and half-past times.
8Shapes & ObjectsRecognise, name and describe 2D shapes and 3D objects. Activities build geometry vocabulary and spatial awareness.
9Data & GraphsCollect, organise and interpret simple data using tally marks, tables and picture graphs. Students compare results using everyday examples.
10MoneyRecognise coins and practise simple money skills through matching, counting and shopping-style activities.

Science

Browse Year 1 Science worksheets by topic.

S.No Topic Description Download
1Living & Non-Living ThingsLearn how to identify living and non-living things. Students explore the basic needs of plants, animals and people.
2PlantsExplore plant parts, seeds, growth and what plants need to survive. Worksheets encourage observation and curiosity about nature.
3AnimalsLearn about animal features, habitats and basic needs. Students compare pets, farm animals, insects, birds and wildlife.
4Human Body & SensesExplore body parts, the five senses, healthy habits and personal care. Activities help students understand how their bodies work.
5Weather & SeasonsIdentify common weather conditions and seasonal changes. Students observe how weather affects clothing, activities and the environment.
6MaterialsInvestigate everyday materials and their properties. Students compare objects by texture, strength, flexibility and use.
7Pushes & PullsIntroduce basic forces through movement activities. Students learn how pushes and pulls can make objects move, stop or change direction.
8Light & SoundExplore light, shadows and sound using simple observations. Students learn how light helps us see and how sounds are made.
9Earth, Sky & SpaceIntroduce the Sun, Moon, stars, day and night. Activities support early understanding of Earth and the sky.
10Science InvestigationsPractise observing, predicting, sorting and recording results. These worksheets help students build early scientific thinking skills.

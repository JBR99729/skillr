Year 1 Worksheets

Explore our printable Year 1 Maths and Science worksheets designed for teachers, parents and homeschool families. Resources cover number sense, place value, addition, subtraction, measurement, patterns, data, living things, materials and science inquiry through engaging classroom-ready activities.

Every worksheet is created to align with the Australian Curriculum while remaining useful for classrooms worldwide. Free sample packs are available for every topic.

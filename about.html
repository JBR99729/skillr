About Skillr Education

Skillr Education is a growing collection of free printable worksheets and learning resources designed to support students, parents and teachers.

Resources are organised by year level, subject, topic and skill to make it easy to find appropriate learning materials.

Our goal is to provide simple, accessible and high-quality educational resources from Foundation through to Year 12.

New worksheets and learning materials are added regularly as the website continues to grow.

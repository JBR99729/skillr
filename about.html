About Skillr Education - Free Curriculum-Aligned Worksheets

Skillr Education provides free printable Maths and Science worksheets for Pre-school to Year 12 students. Our resources are made to support teachers, parents and students with easy-to-use, curriculum-aligned practice materials.

What We Offer

Download worksheet packs organised by year level and subject. All resources are free to use, printer-friendly, and published without subscriptions, ads, sign-ups or student data collection.

  • Free printable Maths and Science worksheets
  • Curriculum-aligned content for early learning and primary school
  • Simple, printer-friendly layout for home and classroom use

How To Use the Site

Find worksheets from the homepage, sitemap, or year pages. We are adding new topics gradually, with a focus on quality and useful learning practice.

Feedback & Support

If you need a worksheet for a specific topic or have suggestions, please contact us at skillrhublearning@gmail.com. Your feedback helps us improve and expand the collection.
